How to Protect Your Online Purchases from Fraud

Online fraud is on the rise. In 2025 alone, consumers lost billions to credit card fraud, phishing scams, and data breaches. Here are practical steps you can take right now to protect yourself.

1. Use Virtual Cards for Every Online Purchase

Virtual cards are the single most effective tool for preventing online fraud. By generating a unique card number for each transaction or merchant, you ensure that even if a merchant is compromised, your real financial details remain safe. Payzoris makes this effortless — create a new card in seconds.

2. Never Save Card Details on Merchant Sites

It's convenient, but risky. If a merchant suffers a data breach, your saved card details are exposed. With virtual cards, you don't need to save anything — generate a new card each time or set up a recurring card for subscriptions.

3. Spot Phishing Attempts

Fraudsters are getting sophisticated. Always check the sender's email address, look for generic greetings, hover over links before clicking, and never enter your payment details on a site you were directed to via email.

4. Monitor Your Transactions Regularly

Payzoris gives you real-time notifications for every transaction. Review your spending daily. The sooner you spot an unauthorized charge, the faster you can freeze the card and prevent further damage.

5. Set Spending Limits

With Payzoris, you can set per-card spending limits. If a card's details are stolen, the thief can only spend up to the limit you set — minimizing your risk exposure.

Stay Safe Out There

Fraud prevention is about building good habits. Combine virtual cards with vigilance, and you'll be light-years ahead of most consumers when it comes to online security.
